"Prof. G. V. K. Sinha: A Legacy of Innovation and Excellence"

Shri G.V.K Sinha, Founder-Lingaya’s Group of Institutions, was born to a freedom fighter in a village near Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. He was a noble soul; his upbringing had taught him to be selfless and eager to help others. Following his graduation from Mechanical Engineering and a brief stint as a field engineer in PWD , Prof. Sinha moved into the field of technical education. Before retiring, he spent 35 years advocating for technical education, and he continued to do so for another 10 years.. The Government of India recognized his technical education knowledge and experience, and he was appointed as a consultant for AICTE, India’s premier professional education authority. He left for his heavenly abode on 6th June 2011. His demise is a significant loss not only to his family but to the entire country. The motherland lost a noble son. Lingayas Institute of Management and Technology (LIMAT), located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, is a private institute established in 2008, offering UG and PG programs in Engineering, IT, and Business & Management Studies, with AICTE accreditation and affiliation with JNTUK, Kakinada.

About the Institute Overview

Lingayas Institute of Management and Technology (LIMT), established in 2008 in Vijayawada, offers quality education in engineering, technology, and management. Affiliated with JNTUK and AICTE-approved, LIMT provides B.Tech and M.Tech programs, including Latest industry required AI-DS and Machine learning courses. The institute has grown from 240 to an intake of 918 per year, adding new programs to meet industry needs.
